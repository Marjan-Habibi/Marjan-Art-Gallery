Sunflowers by Vincent van Gogh

Sunflowers is one of Vincent van Gogh’s most famous paintings. He painted several versions of sunflowers between 1888 and 1889 in France. The painting shows a vase full of bright yellow sunflowers. The flowers are in different stages — some are fresh, and some are dry. Van Gogh used shades of yellow to show warmth, light, and life.
